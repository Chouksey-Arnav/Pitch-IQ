Common Questions

Everything you need to know.

Your video never leaves your device. PitchIQ uses Google MediaPipe Pose, which runs entirely inside your browser as a local JavaScript process. No data is transmitted to any server. The analysis happens on your own computer or phone. You can verify this yourself by turning off your internet connection after the page loads — the analysis still works.
When you watch yourself back, you see what you see — but you don't know what you're looking for. PitchIQ's AI maps the exact angles of your joints and compares them against cricket biomechanical benchmarks. It doesn't just show you the video — it tells you your front elbow is at 98° (too straight), your stance is 140% of shoulder width (too wide), your front arm dropped below shoulder height. Specific numbers. Specific coaching cues. That's the difference.
Your limit resets at midnight local time every night. So if you run 3 analyses on Tuesday, your counter resets at midnight and you have a fresh 3 on Wednesday. If you want unlimited analyses — useful if you're doing serious net sessions or reviewing multiple deliveries from a bowling spell — the Premium plan at $5/month removes all limits.
Any smartphone camera works. The best setup is: side-on angle (directly to your left or right), camera at waist height, about 5–10 metres away. Ask a teammate or lean your phone against a cricket bag. Good, even lighting helps significantly — natural outdoor light is ideal. Wear clothing that contrasts with your background so the AI can detect your body clearly. Loose whites against a white sightscreen is the hardest scenario; a coloured shirt against the outfield is the easiest.
PitchIQ uses the same Google MediaPipe Pose model used in professional sports analysis applications. On good-quality side-on footage in reasonable lighting, landmark detection is highly accurate. Joint angles are calculated mathematically from detected positions. That said, PitchIQ is a supplementary tool, not a replacement for human coaching. Use it to identify patterns, track changes over time, and come to your coaching sessions with specific questions. The combination of AI analysis and a human coach is significantly more powerful than either alone.
Cancel anytime from your account settings — it takes about 10 seconds. No cancellation fees, no "are you sure?" loops, no retention dark patterns. Your premium access continues until the end of your billing period. We respect your time too much to make cancellation difficult.
