Questions

Everything you need to know.

Your video is analyzed entirely within your browser using your own device's processing power. Nothing is ever transmitted to any server. You can verify this by disconnecting Wi-Fi after the page loads — every analysis still works perfectly. We don't have video analysis servers by design. Your footage is yours.
A kinogram captures multiple skeleton poses across a movement and composites them into one image, showing the full motion arc simultaneously. Professional cricket coaches use kinograms to spot inefficiencies invisible in normal video — like a bowler's arm dropping mid-run-up, or weight transfer during a batting stroke. PitchIQ generates kinograms automatically in seconds.
Side-on camera angle, waist height, 5–10m away. Good, even lighting is the single biggest factor — overcast outdoor is ideal. Wear fitted clothing that contrasts with the background. Blue shirt against a green outfield is easy; loose whites against a white sightscreen is hard. Full body in frame with some space at the top and bottom.
